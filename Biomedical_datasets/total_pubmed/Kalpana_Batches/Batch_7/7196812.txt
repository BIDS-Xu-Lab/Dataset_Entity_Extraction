The molecular structure of (+/-)-7 alpha, 8 beta-dihydroxy-7,8-dihydrobenzo[a]pyrene has been determined by X-ray crystallographic methods.
The analysis has shown that the two hydroxyl groups are trans to each other and di-equatorial to the ring.
The dihydrobenzene group adopts a distorted half-chair pucker.
Trends in several bond distances indicate reactive points in the molecule.
