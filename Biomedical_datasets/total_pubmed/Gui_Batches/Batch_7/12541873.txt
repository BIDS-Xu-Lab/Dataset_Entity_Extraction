To study the diagnostic value of audiological and vestibular function examination in the pontocerebellar angle tumor.
The data of audiological and vestibular function test were analized retrospectively in 20 subjects with tumors of the pontocerebellar angle.
Pure tone and ABR waveforms were abnormal in 20 subjects (21 ears), moreover acoustic stapedius reflex and the vestibular function were abnormal in 19 subjects.
It's valuable that audiological and vestibular function examinations are able to diagnosis the pontocerebellar angle tumor.
