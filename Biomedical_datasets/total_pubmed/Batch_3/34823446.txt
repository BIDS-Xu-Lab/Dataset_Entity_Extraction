Falls are the leading cause of injuries and death among the elderly in the United States (U.S.).
This study examined unintentional fall related-injuries and potential associations between various consumer products.
Data was analyzed from the National Electronic Injury Surveillance System (NEISS) of hospital emergency department (ED) visits for unintentional injuries among the elderly between 2007 and 2017.
Multiple logistic regression was used to examine the association between consumer products and fall-related injury ED visits.
